Shipping and Delivery Information

Shipping Options

Standard Shipping
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: $5.99 (FREE on orders over $50)
- Tracking: Provided via email
- Carrier: USPS or regional carrier

Express Shipping
- Delivery time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $14.99
- Tracking: Real-time updates
- Carrier: FedEx or UPS

Overnight Shipping
- Delivery time: Next business day
- Cost: $29.99
- Order by: 2PM EST for next day delivery
- Carrier: FedEx Priority Overnight
- Available: Monday-Thursday only

International Shipping
We ship to over 100 countries worldwide.
- Delivery time: 7-21 business days
- Cost: Calculated at checkout
- Customs: Customer responsible for duties/taxes
- Tracking: International tracking provided

Order Processing
- Processing time: 24 hours for in-stock items
- Warehouse hours: Monday-Friday 8AM-5PM EST
- Weekend orders: Processed next business day
- Holidays: Processing may be delayed

Packaging
All orders are:
- Carefully inspected before shipping
- Packed with protective materials
- Shipped in recyclable packaging
- Include packing slip and return label

Tracking Your Order
1. Check your email for tracking number
2. Visit our tracking page
3. Enter order number or tracking number
4. View real-time location and status
5. Estimated delivery date provided

Delivery Issues
If your package is:
- Lost: File claim after 10 business days
- Damaged: Contact us within 48 hours
- Stolen: Police report required for claim
- Wrong address: Returned to sender, reshipping fees apply

Address Requirements
Ensure your address includes:
- Full street address (no PO Boxes for Express)
- Apartment or unit number
- Correct ZIP/postal code
- Phone number for delivery notifications

Signature Requirements
Required for orders over $500
- Adult signature (21+) for restricted items
- Someone must be present for delivery
- Delivery attempts: 3 maximum
- Hold for pickup available

Holiday Shipping
Peak season considerations:
- Order early for guaranteed delivery
- Extended processing times may apply
- Shipping carriers may experience delays
- Check holiday cutoff dates on website

Free Shipping Eligibility
- Orders over $50 (standard shipping)
- Loyalty program members
- First-time customers (promotional)
- Bulk orders over $500
