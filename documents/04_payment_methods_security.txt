Payment Security

Encryption Standards
All payment information is protected with:
- 256-bit SSL encryption
- PCI DSS Level 1 compliance
- TLS 1.3 protocol
- End-to-end encryption for all transactions

Data Protection
Your payment security:
- No credit card numbers stored on our servers
- Tokenization for saved payment methods
- Two-factor authentication available
- Fraud detection algorithms
- Real-time transaction monitoring

Card Verification
Security measures include:
- CVV/CVC verification required
- Address Verification System (AVS)
- 3D Secure authentication (Verified by Visa, Mastercard SecureCode)
- Unusual activity alerts

Fraud Prevention
We protect against fraud by:
- Monitoring suspicious transactions
- Verifying large or unusual orders
- Checking billing and shipping address matches
- Velocity checks on repeat purchases
- Device fingerprinting technology

Payment Processing

Authorization and Capture
- Authorization: Funds reserved at checkout
- Capture: Charged when order ships
- Pre-authorization: May see temporary hold
- Authorization expires: 7 days if not captured

Payment Holds
Temporary holds may occur:
- First-time customers
- Large order amounts (over $500)
- Shipping to different address than billing
- International orders
- Multiple orders in short timeframe

Failed Payments
If payment doesn't process:
- Check card expiration date
- Verify billing address matches card
- Ensure sufficient funds available
- Contact your bank about international transactions
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact our support team
