Product Warranty Information

Standard Warranty Coverage

All products purchased from our company include a comprehensive warranty covering manufacturing defects and premature failures.

Warranty Duration
- Electronics: 1 year from purchase date
- Appliances: 2 years from purchase date
- Furniture: 5 years on structural components
- Clothing: 90 days on stitching and materials
- Accessories: 6 months from purchase date

What's Covered
The warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects in materials
- Workmanship issues
- Hardware failures during normal use
- Premature wear under normal conditions
- Electrical or mechanical malfunctions

What's NOT Covered
The warranty does not cover:
- Normal wear and tear
- Accidental damage or misuse
- Water or liquid damage
- Unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents)
- Lost or stolen items
- Damage from improper storage
- Acts of nature (fire, flood, earthquake)

Extended Warranty Options

Extended Protection Plan
- Additional 2 years coverage
- Cost: 15% of product price
- Purchase within 30 days of original purchase
- Covers accidental damage
- No deductible for claims

Premium Protection Plan
- Additional 3 years coverage
- Cost: 25% of product price
- Includes accidental damage protection
- Covers battery replacement (electronics)
- Priority repair service
- Loaner devices available

How to File a Warranty Claim

Step 1: Gather Information
- Proof of purchase (receipt or order number)
- Product model and serial number
- Detailed description of issue
- Photos or videos of defect
- Contact information

Step 2: Contact Warranty Department
- Email: warranty@example.com
- Phone: 1-800-WARRANTY
- Online form: www.example.com/warranty
- Include all gathered information

Step 3: Warranty Review
- Claim reviewed within 2 business days
- May request additional information
- Pre-authorization provided for approved claims
- Return shipping label sent via email

Step 4: Return Product
- Pack securely in original packaging if possible
- Include all accessories and documentation
- Attach pre-authorization number to package
- Ship within 10 days of approval

Step 5: Repair or Replacement
- Items inspected within 3-5 business days
- Repaired items returned within 10 business days
- Replacements shipped within 5 business days
- Tracking information provided

Warranty Transfer
- Warranty is transferable to new owner
- Requires proof of original purchase
- Transfer form must be completed
- No fee for transfer
- Remaining warranty period applies

International Warranty
- Valid in country of purchase only
- International repairs may incur fees
