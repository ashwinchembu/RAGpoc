Returns and Refunds Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Overview
At our company, we stand behind the quality of our products and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines our returns and refunds procedures.

Return Window
- Standard items: 30 days from date of purchase
- Electronics and technology: 14 days from date of purchase
- Custom or personalized items: Non-returnable unless defective
- Sale or clearance items: Final sale (unless defective)

Return Conditions
Items must meet the following conditions to be eligible for return:
1. Original condition with all tags attached
2. Unused and unworn (except for trying on)
3. Original packaging included
4. Proof of purchase (receipt or order number)
5. No signs of wear or damage

How to Initiate a Return
1. Log into your account at www.example.com
2. Navigate to "Order History"
3. Select the order and click "Request Return"
4. Choose reason for return
5. Print prepaid return label
6. Pack item securely
7. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refund Processing
- Refund method: Original payment method
- Processing time: 5-7 business days after receipt
- Inspection period: 2-3 business days
- Bank processing: 3-5 additional business days
- Total time: Approximately 10-15 business days

Defective Items
If you receive a defective item:
- Immediate replacement at no cost
- Expedited shipping for replacement
- No return shipping charges
- Contact support within 48 hours of receipt

Exceptions
- Hygiene products (opened)
- Digital downloads
- Gift cards
- Intimate apparel (opened)
- Items marked "Final Sale"

International Returns
International customers are responsible for:
- Return shipping costs
- Any customs duties or taxes
- Currency conversion fees may apply

Contact Information
For return assistance:
- Email: returns@example.com
- Phone: 1-800-RETURNS (24/7)
- Live Chat: Available Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM EST
