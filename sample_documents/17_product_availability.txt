Stock Information and Product Notifications

Real-Time Inventory:
- Stock levels updated every 15 minutes
- Shown on product pages
- "In Stock" = Ships within 24 hours
- "Low Stock" = Less than 10 units
- "Out of Stock" = Currently unavailable
- "Pre-Order" = Coming soon

Stock Indicators:

In Stock:
- Available for immediate shipment
- Order today, ships today (if by 2pm EST)
- Guaranteed availability
- Standard delivery times apply

Low Stock:
- Limited quantities remaining
- First come, first served
- May sell out before order processed
- Consider expedited shipping
- Similar items suggested

Out of Stock:
- Currently unavailable
- Restock date estimated if known
- Sign up for restock notifications
- Similar in-stock alternatives shown
- Backorder option may be available

Pre-Order:
- Product not yet released
- Reserve your item now

- Expected release date shown
- Ships immediately upon release
- Cancel anytime before shipment
