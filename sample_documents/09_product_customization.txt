Care Instructions

Engraved Items
- Clean with soft cloth
- Avoid abrasive cleaners
- Polish as recommended for material
- Engraving is permanent
- Will not fade or wear off

Embroidered Items
- Machine wash cold
- Turn inside out
- Gentle cycle
- Tumble dry low
- Iron on reverse side only

Monogrammed Items
- Follow garment care instructions
- Avoid bleach on monograms
- Dry clean when recommended
- Iron carefully around monogram
- Hand wash delicate items
