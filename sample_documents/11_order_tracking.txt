Tracking Issues

Tracking Not Updating
If tracking shows no movement for 48+ hours:
- Weather delays possible
- Holiday shipping surge
- Carrier scanning issues
- Package may still be moving

What to do:
1. Wait 48 hours from last scan
2. Check carrier website directly
3. Contact carrier customer service
4. Contact our support if needed

Lost Package
Package marked as lost if:
- No scans for 7+ days
- Carrier confirms loss
- Investigation completed

We will:
- Send immediate replacement (in-stock items)
- Issue full refund (out-of-stock items)
- Expedited shipping on replacement
- No cost to you

Damaged Package
If package arrives damaged:
1. Take photos immediately
2. Note damage on delivery receipt
3. Contact us within 48 hours
4. Keep all packaging
5. We'll arrange pickup and replacement

Wrong Address
If you provided wrong address:
- Contact us within 2 hours of order
- Address correction possible before ship
- After shipping: Contact carrier
- Return to sender: Will be reshipped (shipping fees apply)
