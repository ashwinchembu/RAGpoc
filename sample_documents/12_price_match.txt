PRICE MATCH GUARANTEE

Our Commitment
We guarantee you're getting the best price. If you find a lower price on an identical item from a qualifying competitor, we'll match it.

Price Match Terms

Eligible Items
- Identical manufacturer and model number
- Same size, color, and specifications
- In-stock at competitor
- New, not refurbished or used
- Not clearance or closeout items

Qualifying Competitors
- Authorized retailers only
- Online and physical stores
- Must be in United States
- Active retail websites
- Brick-and-mortar locations

Excluded Competitors
- Marketplace sellers (eBay, Amazon third-party)
- Auction sites
- Unauthorized dealers
- Going-out-of-business sales
- Member-only pricing (Costco, Sam's Club)
- Flash sale sites

Time Frame
Price match available:
- Before purchase: At time of order
- After purchase: Within 14 days of delivery
- Provide proof of lower price
- Must be currently available

How to Request Price Match

Before Purchase

Step 1: Find Lower Price
- Locate identical item at competitor
- Verify it's in stock
- Note the price
- Capture screenshot or save link

Step 2: Contact Us
- Call: 1-800-PRICE-MATCH
- Email: pricematch@company.com
- Live chat on website
- Include competitor details

Step 3: Submit Documentation
Provide:
- Competitor name
- Product URL or store location
- Screenshot of price
- Date price verified
- Your contact information

Step 4: Verification
We will verify:
- Item is identical
- Competitor is eligible
- Price is currently available
- Item is in-stock
- Processing time: 4-24 hours

Step 5: Price Adjusted
If approved:
- Price adjusted immediately
- Order at matched price
- Confirmation email sent
- Proceed with purchase

After Purchase

Within 14 Days of Delivery
1. Find lower price at competitor
2. Gather documentation
3. Submit price match claim
4. Verification process
5. Refund issued for difference

Required Information
- Original order number
- Proof of delivery date
- Competitor information
- Current lower price
- Screenshot or link

Refund Process
If approved:
- Difference refunded to original payment
- Processing: 5-7 business days
- Refund confirmation email
- No restocking fees

Price Match Details

Matching Criteria

Total Price
We match:
- Item price
- Not including taxes
- Not including shipping costs
- Not including membership fees
- Base price comparison

Special Pricing Exclusions
We do not match:
- Bundle deals (unless exact same bundle)
- Buy-one-get-one offers
- Percentage off entire purchase
- Coupon codes or promotions
- Rebates or mail-in offers
- Loyalty program pricing
- Employee discounts
- Student/military discounts (see our own programs)

Identical Product
Must match:
- Manufacturer
- Model number
- Size/dimensions
- Color/finish
- Included accessories
- Warranty terms
- Generation/year

Documentation Requirements

Acceptable Proof
- Website screenshot with date
- Link to product page
- Physical store receipt
- Store ad or catalog
- Price tag photo (with store visible)

Screenshot Must Show
- Competitor name
- Product details
- Current price
- In-stock status
- Current date
- Full web address

Physical Store
For brick-and-mortar stores:
- Store name and location
- Photo of price tag
- Store ad with date
- Receipt from store visit (if verification shopping)

Verification Process

Our Verification
We will check:
- Competitor authorization status
- Product availability
- Price accuracy
- Terms and conditions
- Shipping costs

Verification Time
- Standard: 4-24 hours
- Rush service: 2-4 hours (+$10 fee)
- Complex requests: Up to 48 hours
- Notification when complete

Reasons for Denial
Request may be denied if:
- Product not identical
- Competitor not eligible
- Out of stock at competitor
- Price no longer available
- Documentation insufficient
- Excluded product category

Appeal Process
If request denied:
- Contact price match team
- Provide additional documentation
- Manager review available
- Final decision within 48 hours

Limitations

Per Customer
- Unlimited price matches allowed
- Each request evaluated individually
- Good faith requirement
- Abuse may result in restriction

Per Product
- One price match per item
- If competitor lowers price again, new request required
- Cannot combine with other promotions
- Some exclusions apply
